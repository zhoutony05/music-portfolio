About Me

Tony lives in Los Gatos, California and currently is a Senior attending Los Gatos High School. Tony has a passion for music, playing both the piano and the violin. Tony started his piano lesson with Dr. Grace Long when he was 5. He won his first competition at RMTA Jazz festival at age of 6. Since then, he has developed into a talented and hardworking musician. Tony’s most recent piano competition awards include 1st Prize winner at 2021 International Music Competition “Rome” Grand Prize Virtuoso; 1st Place winner at US International Open Music Competition, 2nd Place winner at American Protégé International Piano and String Competition, etc. Tony is currently studying piano with Lin Pan. Outside of his competitions, Tony shares his passion for music with others by volunteering and playing music for children with special needs, something he thinks has impacted his life in a positive way.
